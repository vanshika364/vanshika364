## 🍴 Featured Project: ForkCast

<p align="center">
  <a href="https://fork-cast-lilac.vercel.app">

**ForkCast** was designed to reduce the daily struggle of deciding where and what to eat.

The product helps users make more personalized dining decisions based on their:

🥗 Nutrition goals
💸 Budget
📍 Location
❤️ Preferences
⏰ Current context

My contributions included:

* Conducting user research and customer interviews
* Designing and testing a concierge MVP
* Analyzing user behavior and feedback
* Helping shape the recommendation experience
* Iterating on product features through experiments
* Supporting product strategy and customer validation
